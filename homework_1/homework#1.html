• 450g (1 lb) beef mince
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small green pepper, diced
  • 2 (400g) tins chopped tomatoes
  • 1 (500g) jar passata
  • 150g tomato puree
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  1. Prep:15min
  2. Cook:1hr10min
  3. 1hr25min
  4. Combine mince, onion, garlic and green pepper in a large saucepan. Cook and stir until meat is brown and vegetables are tender. Drain fat from pan.
  5. Stir chopped tomatoes, passata and tomato puree into the pan. Season with oregano, basil, salt and pepper. Simmer pasta sauce for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
  6. Serve
